AMC SERVICES

SOLAR SYSTEM

A solar system converts sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic panels, supported by inverters, batteries, and controllers, providing renewable, cost-effective energy for homes and businesses, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.

ONLINE SYSTEM

An online solar system integrates photovoltaic panels with the grid, using inverters and smart controllers to provide renewable energy, reduce electricity bills, and allow excess power to be sold back to the grid.

LIFT BACKUP SYSTEM

A lift backup system ensures continuous elevator operation during power outages, using batteries or generators to provide emergency power, enhancing safety and reliability in residential and commercial buildings.

ALL TYPES OF BATTERIES

UPS BATTERY

A UPS battery provides backup power during outages, ensuring uninterrupted operation of electronic devices and critical systems, commonly using lead-acid or lithium-ion technology for reliability and efficiency.

SOLAR BATTERY

A solar battery stores energy generated by solar panels, enabling power usage during non-sunny periods, increasing energy independence, and optimizing solar system efficiency by providing reliable backup and load shifting.

SMF BATTERY

An SMF (Sealed Maintenance-Free) battery is a type of lead-acid battery with a sealed design, requiring no water addition, offering reliable, maintenance-free power storage for various applications like UPS and solar systems.

LITHIUM BATTERY

A lithium battery is a rechargeable energy storage device known for high energy density, long lifespan, and fast charging, commonly used in electronics, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems.
